The Senior Pledge

What it is, why it matters, and how you can help

🦄

What is the Senior Pledge?

As we wrap up our final semester and reflect on the opportunities we've had as Unicorns, the Senior Pledge is our way of giving back to NCSSM. By pledging, you commit to:

  • Stay connected through the NCSSM Alumni Network
  • Promote NCSSM to prospective students
  • Donate $5/year for 5 years after graduation
🎓

The '26 Club

Every senior who makes the pledge becomes a member of the '26 Club and receives a custom NCSSM-Durham Class of 2026 vinyl laptop sticker. Members are also honored on NCSSM's website.

🍦

180 Pledges — Ice Cream Social

If 180 seniors (50% of our class) make the pledge, we celebrate with a class ice cream social and turn Chancellor Roberts into a human sundae!

💰

209 Pledges — $5,000 Challenge Gift

If 209 seniors make the pledge, we unlock a $5,000 Senior Pledge Challenge Gift to fund the need area of our choice.

❤️

Where Does It Go?

When you pledge, you choose which area of need you'd like the Challenge Gift to support:

  • Academic Counseling
  • Sports and Academic Competitions
  • J-Term Travel
  • Mental Health Counseling
